Clinical Trial Analytics

Safety review in days, not weeks

3 wksto a few days
1,000+events reviewed each cycle

A large pharmaceutical sponsor reviewed safety data once a month, working by hand through more than a thousand adverse events. Important signals could sit unnoticed for weeks.

We built an interactive review application with daily data refresh, statistical signal detection, and the ability to drill from a signal down to the individual patient. The safety team now sees emerging issues in days.
